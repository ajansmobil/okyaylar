User Rights

Everyone has the right to request the protection of their personal data. The protection of your personal data is very important to us, and we act within the framework of the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) No. 6698 and related legislation. Users have the right to be informed about the purposes for which their data is collected, how it is stored, and with whom it is shared.

You can request to update, correct, delete, and anonymize your personal data. You can also request detailed information about the purpose for which the collected data is used and the processing process, and obtain information about third parties involved in these processes. You can always contact us to exercise your rights.

You can review the Privacy Policy on our website for detailed information about the protection of your personal data and your rights. As a user, the security of your data is our priority, and we have taken the necessary technical and administrative measures.
