Our Privacy Policy

When you visit our website, certain information is collected through cookies to improve your use of our site and the services offered. This information, excluding your personal identification details, is used to better provide the services on our site and improve user experience. Cookies are used to remember your preferences, optimize the site's functionality, and provide you with a better experience.

The collected information is not shared with third parties and is used solely to improve our site's performance and develop our services. Users can block the use of cookies by changing their browser settings. However, please note that some parts of our site may not function properly in this case.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We take necessary technical and administrative measures to ensure the security of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, you can contact us.
